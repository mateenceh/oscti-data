Managing Risk In Times of Disruption: How RSA Archer Customers Rise to the Challenge

Oct 06, 2020 | by RSA |

Nearly a year since the global pandemic outbreak, has your definition of "risk" changed?

For many businesses, the GRC function is becoming a critical business enabler and a foundational element of the organization's future operations. ITSPmagazine's Sean Martin and Marco Ciappelli spoke with Paige Sundquist, IT Risk Management and Data Privacy Advisor, FedEx, and Gopal Padinjaruveetil, Chief Information Security Officer (CISO), The Auto Club Group (AAA) of Michigan,  to understand the future of the risk management landscape.
