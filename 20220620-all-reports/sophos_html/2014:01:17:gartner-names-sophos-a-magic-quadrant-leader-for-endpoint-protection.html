Gartner names Sophos a Magic Quadrant Leader for Endpoint Protection

Corporate•Enduser•Awards•Cloud•Endpoint Protection•Reviews
17 January 2014
  • 4
  • Share on Twitter
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on LinkedIn
By John Zorabedian

For the sixth consecutive year, Gartner has positioned Sophos as a Leader in the Magic Quadrant for Endpoint Protection Platforms. Sophos is the only IT security company that’s a Leader in the Magic Quadrants for Unified Threat Management (UTM), Mobile Data Protection and Endpoint Protection Platforms.

We believe that we’re a Leader once again because we continue to innovate how we deliver endpoint protection: from integrating UTM and endpoint into a single console—to including mobile device management (MDM) and endpoint as a single, per-user license—and offering Sophos Cloud, providing Security Made Simple that’s painless to deploy and easy to manage. Our endpoint protection covers end users, wherever they are and whatever devices they use, in a way that’s fast, effective, and easy.

The Magic Quadrant is based on Gartner’s assessment of a company’s ability to execute and completeness of vision. The report gives you independent research into the endpoint protection market, explains what’s happening in this segment of the security industry, and offers strengths and cautions for each of the vendors.

Get the full report to find out what Gartner says about why we’re a Leader (registration required).

Simply Winning

Our endpoint, mobile and cloud products have won accolades from many independent reviewers, analysts and publications. Sophos Endpoint Protection received the highest mark in a recent performance test from AV-Comparatives; and AV-Comparatives also recognized our endpoint product as well-suited for small businesses.

Sophos Mobile Control, our mobile device management product, recently won the 2013 Best Enterprise Security Solution of the Year award from MuyComputer. Sophos Cloud has also garnered strong reviews from customers, partners and industry analysts such as IDC.
