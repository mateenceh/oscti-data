XG Firewall v17. Sleep easy.

Corporate•Network•Synchronized Security•XG Firewall•XG Firewall v17

v17 combines a breakthrough in network visibility with improvements to our industry leading protection and outstanding user experience. It could be the firewall of your dreams.

24 October 2017 By Chris McCormack
  • 2
  • Share on Twitter
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on LinkedIn

At Sophos, we’re all about making IT security simpler and more effective. How easy? Easy enough that satisfied customer Philip Petrow was moved to say:

I can confidently say that I sleep better at night because of it.

Well Philip, get ready to sleep like a baby because today we’re releasing v17 of our XG Firewall.

Our XG Firewalls have always had your back, giving you the control you need for a good night’s sleep. Version 17 is something special, though. On top of improvements to our industry leading protection and outstanding user experience, it delivers a breakthrough in network visibility.

It could be the firewall of your dreams.

A new level of visibility and control

XG Firewall introduces Synchronized App Control, a breakthrough that delivers absolute clarity and control over networked applications.

It extends our revolutionary Synchronized Security technology that connects your endpoints and firewall with a Security Heartbeat™ to share important information about health status, threat intelligence, and now network application traffic. It takes application visibility and control to a whole new level:

Network Security Made Simple

With every release of XG Firewall we try to make your job easier.

Well, we’ve done it again.

Version 17 includes a raft of things to simplify day-to-day management, while improving flexibility, power and effectiveness:

  • An all-new deployment wizard dramatically streamlines deployment and setup.
  • Firewall rule management enhancements make it much easier to manage large firewall rule set.s
  • A unified log viewer and more granular logging give you the insight you need to troubleshoot quickly.
  • The Policy Test Simulator makes it easy to check firewall rules and policy settings.
  • NAT rule and object enhancements enable intuitive but powerful object definitions and NAT rules.
  • IPS and app control policy improvements make custom policies easier to build and maintain.

Top Rated Security Effectiveness and Price-Performance

XG Firewall has been a leader in Gartner’s Magic Quadrant for UTM for the last six years in a row. It’s also received top recognition from industry experts like NSS Labs where it’s been rated among the highest for security effectiveness, performance, and value.

XG Firewall v17 further extends our protection, value and performance advantage with our new class-leading XG Series hardware appliances.

The new XG Series appliances offer improved performance, expanded connectivity, and added resiliency and redundancy options. Equipped with the latest Intel CPUs, generous RAM, and integrated solid-state storage, our XG Series appliances offer a compelling price-performance ratio.

Built-in bypass port pairs enable new deployment options with continued traffic flow even when the device is offline and Power over Ethernet (PoE) FleXi Port modules enable easy deployment of VoIP or Wireless solutions and redundant power supply options in all our 1U series appliances, offers added business continuity.

With all the networking, VPN, web, and app control enhancements in v17, along with the introduction of machine learning technology into Sophos Sandstorm sandboxing, these new XG Series appliances provide a formidable network security solution that can keep up with the most demanding networks and the latest unknown threats.

Adding XG Firewall and Synchronized Security to Your Network

Dan Russell, CIO at Pine Cove Consulting is another sound sleeper, thanks to XG Firewall’s easy deployment:

Deployment flexibility has made it easy to improve security without the complication of replacing current infrastructure

Adding Synchronized Security to your network couldn’t be easier. You can deploy XG Firewall:

  1. As your primary firewall and get the full benefits of XG Firewall’s unrivalled security, simplicity, and insight.
  2. Inline with your existing firewall and utilize the new bypass ports for a risk-free addition to your network that delivers all the benefits of Synchronized Security.
  3. In Discover Mode (TAP Mode) by simply connecting it to a mirror port on your network switch so it can monitor network activity and provide insights, visibility and reporting in a completely unobtrusive way.

XG Firewall works with Intercept X or Central Endpoint Advanced to provide a complete Synchronized Security Solution.

Intercept X also deploys alongside your existing desktop AV or endpoint solution meaning that you can deploy Sophos Synchronized Security in any environment without replacing any of the existing IT infrastructure (Sync App Control support for Central Endpoint Advanced is also coming soon).

Regardless of how you choose to deploy XG Firewall in your environment, you’ll rest easy thanks to the added visibility and insight it gives you.

Find out more at Sophos.com/xgfirewall.
