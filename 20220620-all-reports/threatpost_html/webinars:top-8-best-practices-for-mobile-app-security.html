Top 8 Best Practices for Mobile App Security

Learn how our experts get a handle on mobile risk and build a secure mobile strategy, one app at a time.

GoToWebinar

Start Date:September 14, 2020

Share:

Your employees’ mobile apps represent one of the best avenues of attack on the enterprise for cybercriminals. That’s because when it comes to creating compelling mobile experiences, application developers are all about perfecting the experience and user interface — not necessarily security. Cybercriminals know this and are increasingly targeting poorly secured apps, leading to malware infestations, data breaches, and legal and regulatory trouble for businesses.

But there are ways to fight back: our experts discuss how to get a handle on mobile risk and build a secure mobile strategy, one app at a time.

Panelists will include:

Dr. Tim Vidas, Senior Distinguished Engineer, Office of the CTO, Secureworks
Vidas, in addition to his role at Secureworks, has served as Team Lead for DARPA’s Cyber Grand Challenge Framework and Architecture team, served at CERT, was the builder of the DEF CON Capture the Flag Competition and more.
Michael Tiffany, Co-founder and President, White Ops
Tiffany, in addition to his role at White Ops, is a Technical Fellow of DARPA-funded Critical Assets Labs, a member of white-hat hacking group ninjas.org and an advisor for the Signal Media Project.
Moderator: Tara Seals, Senior Editor, Threatpost
